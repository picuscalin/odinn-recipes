Pasta Bolognesse

Pasta Bolognesse made the traditional way

Pasta Bolognesse is a dish specific to the Bologna region in Italy.

It is an often times simplified dish such is the case with the canned or school lunch versions but today we will take a look at its traditional form

Ingridients

  • ground beef-500g
  • tomatto piure-700g
  • about 3 tomattoes
  • beed stock
  • salt pepper oregano thyme and basil
  • a box of spaghetti
  • garlic
  • onion
  • red wine
  • carrot

Steps

  1. cook your ground beef tomattoes and garlic
  2. prepare a large saucepan where you will add the stock piure and tomattoes
  3. add the ground beef into the saucepan along with some red wine and let simmer for two hours
  4. at the 1:20 mark start cooking the pasta in salted water
