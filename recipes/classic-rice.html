classic rice

The most simple side

This is a must have recipe for pretty much anyone as rice is one of the most common sides

To one's surprise this recipe is highly customizable

Ingridients

  • long grain rice
  • water
  • salt
  • paprika
  • turmeric

Steps

  1. use a full cup to measure the rice and transfer it to a strainer
  2. put it under a jet of water for 1 minute
  3. transfer rice into a pot and add 1.5 water x its quantity.Since we used a full cup that means a cup and a half
  4. add condiments(can be of ur choice)
  5. once you hear it boiling turn the heat on low and leave to simmer for 10 minutes
